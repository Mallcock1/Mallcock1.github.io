I am a PhD student in the Solar Physics and Space Plasma Research Centre in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Sheffield.

My research is in magnetohydrodynamics (MHD) and its application to Solar magnetic phenomena. I am interested in using analytical results in MHD wave theory in combination with ground and space based observations to understand more about the medium and small-scale magnetic structure of the Sun through a technique known as solar magneto-seismology. For more information about my research, click here.

At the Univerisity of Sheffield I am involved with undergraduate teaching. For more information, click here.

Please feel free to contact me for more information or if you are interested in working with me.
