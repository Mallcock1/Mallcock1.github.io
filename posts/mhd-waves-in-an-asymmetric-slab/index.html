MHD waves in an asymmetric slab

Matthew Allcock

2017-01-16 08:52

Comments

Source

In our latest paper, R. Erdelyi and I give a complete account of the transverse magnetoacoustic waves that can propagate along an asymmetric isolated magnetic slab. This work builds upon previous work on symmetric magnetic slabs (Roberts etc.), makes an interesting analogy to a classical mechanical spring-mass system, and discusses the potential application to magnetic field diagnostics as a tool in solar magneto-seismology.
