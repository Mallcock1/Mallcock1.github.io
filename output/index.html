I am a PhD student in the Solar Physics and Space Plasma Research Centre in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Sheffield, funded by the University Prize Scholarship.


Research

My research is in magnetohydrodynamics (MHD) and its application to Solar phenomena. In particular I am interested in:

  • MHD wave theory,
  • Solar atmospheric heating,
  • Solar magneto-seismology.

Teaching

  • MAS115 Mathematical Investigational Skills (Python, R, Latex, and html),
  • MAS153/159 Mathematics for Chemistry (sets, calculus, limits, differential equations, etc.).
